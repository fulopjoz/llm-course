Required structure

Title:

1. Objective
What question are we trying to answer?

2. Data used
What dataset, columns, and sample size did we use?

3. Method
What did we inspect manually? What did AI help with?

4. Findings
What patterns were visible in the data?

5. Limitations
What makes the conclusion uncertain?

6. Recommendation
What should the team do next?

7. AI-use log
Which tool did we use, what did we ask, and what did we manually check?

Writing rules

Use cautious language such as: In this sample, The data suggests, This would need verification, and The next step should be.

Avoid: This proves, AI found the truth, or broad claims about health without criteria.

Final self-check

  • Can I explain the result without reading AI text?
  • Did I manually verify the main claim?
  • Did I mention limitations?
  • Did I describe how AI was used?
  • Would a non-technical teammate understand the decision?
