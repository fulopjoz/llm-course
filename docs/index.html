AI for student projects

From messy information to reliable decisions. A beginner-first WebExpo transfer activity for UCT Prague Technology Skills for Project Managers.

Beginner-first: students do not need GitHub, Python, Codex, Claude Code, Copilot, Cursor, or paid tools. The required route uses a spreadsheet, one AI chat assistant, and a CSV file.
