Goal

Learn to inspect a dataset before asking AI to analyze it. The mistake to avoid is uploading data and trusting the first answer.

Manual inspection questions

  1. How many rows are there?
  2. Which categories appear most often?
  3. Which products have high sugar?
  4. Which products have high salt?
  5. Which products have high saturated fat?
  6. Are drinks and solid foods directly comparable?
  7. Are all countries represented equally?
  8. Are all fields complete?

Comparison problems

  • Sodas and chocolate bars are different product types.
  • Drinks and solid foods may not be comparable in the same way.
  • A small sample can overrepresent one country or category.
  • Product categories can be inconsistent or broad.
